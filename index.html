SPEDOOKER STUDIOS

Spedooker Studios is a small indie game development team formed by a group of friends passionate about creating fun, memorable experiences. Most of our projects are built through game jams, where creativity and experimentation take center stage. We focus on stylish pixel art games with strong atmosphere, charming visuals, and simple gameplay that’s easy to pick up while still being engaging and satisfying to play.

PROJECTS:

Astropoly

Astropoly is a relaxing space mining game where you drill through asteroids, collect valuable resources, upgrade your ship, and unlock new regions of space. Built for a mock game jam, it combines simple gameplay with a chill sci-fi atmosphere and satisfying progression.
